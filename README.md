# Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

The Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard provides insights into retail business performance by analyzing sales, profit, customer segments, and regional trends. The project uses data analysis and visualization techniques to understand key business metrics and support data-driven decision making.

## Technologies Used

* **Python** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
* **SQL** – Data analysis and querying
* **Tableau** – Interactive dashboard and visualization

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard provides several business insights, including:

* **Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity KPIs**
* **Top 10 Products by Profit**
* **Sales by Sub-Category**
* **Profit by Shipping Mode**
* **Sales by State (Geographical Analysis)**
* **Discount Level vs Profit Analysis**
* **Customer Segmentation using RFM**
* **Profit Distribution by Customer Segment**
* **Sales Performance by Region**

## Key Insights

* The **West region** generates the highest overall sales compared to other regions.
* The **Consumer segment** contributes the largest share of total profit, followed by Corporate and Home Office segments.
* **Phones and Chairs** are among the top-performing product sub-categories in terms of sales.
* **Standard Class shipping mode** generates the highest profit margin compared to other shipping modes.
* **High discount levels** tend to reduce profit margins, indicating the need for balanced pricing strategies.
* **Regular customers (RFM analysis)** contribute the majority of sales, showing strong repeat purchasing behavior.
* Certain states such as **California and Washington** show significantly higher sales compared to other states.
* **Office Supplies and Technology categories** contribute significantly to overall sales performance.
* Monthly sales trends indicate **steady business growth across the selected years**.
